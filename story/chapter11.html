Chapter 11: Dress

Two days later…

The Royal Advisor enters Hina’s room upon her request. “Preparations are nearly complete. Tomorrow will be your Coronation.”

“Very well,” Hina nods with a smile on her face. “I wonder, what would I call the subjects when the Coronation arrives?”

“I don’t know what to make of that, but it can be your personal preference,” he said. “Do you have anything on your mind?”

“Hm.”

Hina begins to think of a particular nickname for the subjects. She thought, “I think something cute may be nice, since my palette constitutes cuteness.”

“Oh I know,” she snaps her fingers. “Can you have Agatha in one of the gardens after the Coronation? I think I found the perfect nickname that will suit her.”

“At ease, Oujo-sama.”

…

I wander outside the castle, wearing one of the dresses I had in my wardrobe. For some reason, I realized that I have another pin placed beside the Lotus on its left. Again in colors of green and pink, it was a Sakura flower.

One of the guards -- in which I somehow remember from the time with the delinquents. I gave him a little wave from where I stood, in which he waved back with a smile on his face.

“Glad he’s doing fine,” I thought.

Immediately, I made my way upwards inside, where Hina and the maids were waiting for me. “Glad you had some fresh air,” she smiles. “The Coronation is tomorrow, so we’re gearing up the preparations to the max.”

“Glad to hear that.”

“Also, I personally requested the Royal Tailor to make you an elegant dress for the occasion. I hope you’ll like it.”

“But, what about the dress from the other day?”

“Call it a casual one,” she shrugs. “It’s somehow not intended for such important occasions, though.”

“That’s fair.”

“You can keep it, though,” she said with a grin. “I hope you’ll like it.”

“Thanks!”

At the moment, I didn’t know that Hina was looking at me with a smile on her face, the kind that makes one’s heart flutter. She seems to have bigger plans in mind.

…

Later that evening…

The Royal Advisor is in the room with me. “What brings you here, sir… ?”

“Call me the Royal Advisor,” he said, carrying a large box. “Oujo-sama requested that I give the dress to you.”

I take the box from his hand. “I’m assuming the dress was the one she requested from the Royal Tailor, correct?”

“Yes, it would seem.”

“Thanks,” I said as I unboxed the royal garments to be used for tomorrow. By appearance, it was adorned in shades of pink and green. The frills were covered in glitter and fur, and the skirt was shaded in black.

“But… what’s so special about this?” I thought.

Inside the box, however, there’s a note which I can recognize as Hina's handwriting. It says:

≈≈≈

Agatha,

I hope you like the new dress! I’m actually a bit worried since the Royal Tailor told me earlier that when he’s about to finish the dress, it appears to be already finished together with a note attached to it.

It reads that “as soon as the Coronation ends, something special will happen to the dress”.

To be honest, I’m kinda scared about what will happen, but let’s hope it’s not what I thought it would be.

But still, you can choose to keep the dress if you want to.

Hina

≈≈≈

“Well,” I blurt out. “There’s no harm in trying, right?”

Together with some courage, I finally let out a sigh as I tried to put the dress on. “Let’s see what happens on Coronation Day.”
