Chapter 9: Memories

A day later…

I made my way towards the place where Hina and I were supposed to meet -- one of her gardens. “Where could she be?” I thought.

From afar, I can see Hina panting as she runs in my direction. “Sorry I’m late,” she said.

“There’s nothing to apologize for,” I hushed it off. “Rather, why did you take me to this place?”

Regaining her breath, she calmly walks down towards a row full of young plants. “Here,” she said, handing me a planter. “This is one of the saplings of the Cherry Blossom. Someone gave a whole bag of seeds and this one to me some time ago.”

“That looks beautiful,” I gleam at the sapling. “Where are the others?”

“They’re right over there,” she glances at the bench in response, where a number of planters and their saplings of a similar kind were placed.

“Wow.”

Carefully placing the planter back to its original spot, Hina slowly holds my hand. “You know, I wanted to ask you a little question so I decided that we meet up here.”

“Why? You could ask me some in your room, though.”

“It’s tranquil here,” she said with a slow nod. “I like the atmosphere of this place, it makes me wanna read a book without dealing with the outside noise. And the planters that we saw had a significance to it.”

I nod. “Okay, what was it?”

“So… yesterday, I found a box on top of one of the drawers. They contain three bracelets, a photo frame, and three pins of varying symbols.”

“A… box?”

“It’s not just an ordinary box, as far as I can tell,” she shakes her head. “So I had to ask the Grand Lady about it. She told me that all will be in place when the so-called ‘First Maria’ will then be able to rekindle my heart once more.”

“Sounds like a dream, if you ask me.”

“But the question is… who’s the First Maria?”

I shrug. “I have no idea. Whatever that was, though, might give us a clue on what’s going on.”

“Yeah, I could bet on that part.”

I think for a moment. “Hina may be right about the ‘First Maria’ thing, but… how did I put myself into that picture? I’m sure only the Grand Lady knows what was going on.”

…

Back at the castle…

I was already in her room together with the Oujo-sama -- Hina. She appears to be distraught, and in some sort of confused state that I had to calm her down every few minutes. “Is everything alright?”

“I’m good,” she said, calming down for a bit.

Immediately, I pour a glass of water. “Here, drink this.”

“Thanks,” she said as she gulped down the water. “I’m a little okay now.”

“But, you need to rest,” I declare. “You’re not in a proper state to do anything, and we also need to prepare for your Coronation soon.”

“Yeah, you’re right. But…”

What came afterwards was a question that I didn’t expect to hear coming from her. Is she okay to ask me such a huge favor?
