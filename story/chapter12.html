Chapter 12: Coronation

The Coronation Day has arrived. Immediately, I get out of bed, only to realize that the maids are waiting for me outside the room.

“Agatha-sama, your carriage is waiting,” they said. “Oujo-sama departed early to meet with the subjects and to prepare for the ceremony.”

I nod, following the maids to the carriage as they declared.

As we wandered around the vicinity, I saw children placing assortments of flowers along the sidewalk. Alongside that, their parents were busy cooking some delicious delicacies as they prepared some clothes to be used during the Coronation.

At some point, Hina’s carriage stopped at a nearby flower store as the owner gave her some exquisite flowers in exchange for a few pins made from the Royal Jewellers. I guess Hina’s really popular all over the place, even when she’s not a Queen yet.

I then remember the first time that she told me about the “favor” to which I accepted dearly. To be honest, even after I made my decision, I still don’t know if I would be able to keep up with her pace considering how dynamic she is in terms of her workloads.

“I’m sure she’s looking forward to working with you fully,” one of the maids said with a smile on her face. “She can be serious at times, but she’s doing her best to make us all feel at ease. Even after what had just happened to us, she shrugged it off like it’s no big deal. That goes to show how easy she is when you begin to realize how fun it is to work for her.”

I sigh in relief. “Yeah… I hope it stays there.”

As we head back to the castle, we see a number of people inside the castle preparing some things. “I actually have a little question,” I blurt out.

“Yes?”

I turned to the maid who responded to my query. “Where is… Ouji-sama?”

“Oh,” she fiddles her fingers. “He’s at the chapel helping with stuff. If you want, we can introduce you to him.”

“Please do.”

As we head to the chapel as per my request, we encounter the Royal Bishop, as well as a few others who were cleaning up the chapel. I turn my attention to the Ouji-sama, who’s in the middle of shining the crown and scepter to be used at the Coronation.

“Ouji-sama,” I bow in front of him following the greeting. He seems pleased.

“So you must be the Royal Guest that Oujo-sama was talking about,” he said in a calm tone. “Nice to meet you.”

“So do I.”

“The Coronation should begin in a few moments,” he said. “We’re almost ready here at the chapel. I’m almost done with shining the scepter to be used during the Coronation.”

“Very well,” I nod. “Please take care of yourself after the event.”

“You too.”

As we head out of the chapel, the trumpets begin to tune, signaling that the Coronation is just moments away.

“I think we’re ready,” I said. Tending to the dress that Hina gave me, I pull the pins from the pocket, and put them on the topmost-right part of the bodice.

…

Moments later…

The maids and I are on the front row seat as the Royal Bishop enters the podium. Putting the Holy Bible on top of it, he begins his Coronation speech. Everyone stood to pledge their allegiance to the Queen, to which I participated as well -- not noticing that the Sakura pin faintly started to glow.

The parade started afterwards -- with everyone gazing backwards to witness the coming of the Queen. The Hina that I first saw a few days ago was glittering in her regal black dress. There’s a crown on her head, though I’m not sure what it's for. Would it interfere with the new crown that she’s about to wear?

She stops for a bit, looking to the right -- where the Ouji-sama was waiting along the aisle. Arms linked, they continue walking to the podium.

The Royal Bishop then takes the crown from its place, and slowly places it over her head -- signifying that a new Queen has been reigned. “All hail Hina!”

Everyone chanted afterwards, “All hail Hina!”

Handing the pillow to her direction, he instructed her to carry the scepter. Facing the audience, she proclaimed in a serious but confident tone: “I hereby pledge to the Oath of the Holy Bible to protect the people to whom I cherished. In accordance; I, Hina Oujo, begin my duties as the newly-crowned Queen.”

From then, I realized that the glow began to intensify, so I tried to cover it off with my hand. She notices the ordeal, but then realizes after a full minute of fixating with the audience that her pin -- for some unknown reason I realized was in there in the first place -- also began to glow.

As the cheers went on, so was the applause from inside the chapel. I’m a bit nervous, though.

However, the party came and went without issues. I opted to head out to bed early as I need to check on a few things before I start my job as her Royal Accomplice.
